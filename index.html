PAZ & Partner Fat-Loss Lifestyle Plan 🫡

A sustainable, no-counting, no-starvation daily structure for both of you.
Designed for long-term success, minimal prep, and maximum flavor—together.

🗓️ Daily Meal Plan (Shared Structure)

👨 PAZ Portion Guide (240 lbs)

  • Protein: Palm × 2 (~5–6 oz)
  • Rice/Potato: ½–¾ cup (1 cupped hand)
  • Veggies: 1–2 cups
  • Eggs: 2
  • Yogurt: ¾ cup
  • Fruit: 1 piece (apple, banana, berries)
  • Nuts: 8–10 almonds or walnuts

👩 Nuha Portion Guide (160 lbs)

  • Protein: Palm × 1.5 (~3–4 oz)
  • Rice/Potato: ⅓–½ cup
  • Veggies: 1–2 cups (same)
  • Eggs: 1–2
  • Yogurt: ½–¾ cup
  • Fruit: 1 piece
  • Nuts: 6–8 almonds or walnuts

🍬 Sweet Tooth Survival Rules (Both)

  1. One treat window daily (after lunch or dinner): small, satisfying portion.
  2. Make it rich and enjoyable—chocolate, cookie, or scoop of ice cream.
  3. Use swaps: dates + PB, yogurt + honey, protein pudding.
  4. Keep sweets out of reach—no countertop temptation.
  5. Bonus trick: Brush your teeth after dinner.

🍳 Weekly Prep & Cooking Tips

  • Batch grill proteins (chicken, beef, salmon, boiled eggs).
  • Use steamable veggie bags or chopped salad kits.
  • Keep carbs easy: ready rice packs, air-fried potatoes, pita.
  • Add flavor: garlic, cumin, paprika, lemon, tahini, yogurt sauces.

Stick to the structure. Cook together. Win together. 🫡
